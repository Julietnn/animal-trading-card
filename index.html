CHEETAH

  • length: 3.7feet
  • weight: 140pounds
  • tall: 30inches
  • lifespan: 8-10years
  • habitat: grasslands,savanah

Cheetahs are known for their incredible speed and agility. are the fastest land animals, capable of reaching speeds up to 60 to 70 miles per hour (97 to 113 kilometers per hour) in short bursts covering distances up to 500 meters. Their slender, lightweight build, distinctive black tear stripes on their faces, and non-retractable claws contribute to their unique characteristics.
